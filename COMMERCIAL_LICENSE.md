## Summary

- Non-production use is allowed under `BUSL-1.1`.

- Copies/versions that were already distributed under the prior MIT license remain under MIT for those copies/versions.

## When a paid subscription is required

A commercial subscription is required when `nss-ingestor` is used:
- by a company, government, non-profit, or other organization in production;
- for internal business operations in production;
- to provide managed services, consulting services, or customer-facing services.

## Subscription model

- Billing model: monthly subscription
- Scope: per organization (and as otherwise agreed in a commercial order form)
- Support and SLA: only included with a commercial subscription

Open a private GitHub issue in this repository to request commercial licensing and monthly pricing.
